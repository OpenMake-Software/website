Switching to the Community Knowledgebase

 

You can switch from a Local Knowledgebase to a Community Knowledgebase by changing the OPENMAKE_SERVER entry and restarting the application.  The Community Knowledgebase enables developers, project managers and SCM administrators to share a centralized Knowledgebase where all Workflows and Workflows’ Logs and Reports are stored.  This provides a single repository for the whole community to share Workflow knowledge.

 

 

Table SI-11 Changing the OPENMAKE_SERVER

Step

Action

1

Launch DDS

2

Go to, Window > Preferences

3

Expand Openmake and select Machine

4

Go to the Client Tab

5

In the OPENMAKE_SERVER field, enter in the URL for the Community Knowledgebase

6

Restart the application
