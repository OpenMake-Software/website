Email Notifications in Your Workflow

 

It is recommended that your Workflow use some level of notification to broadcast to users the status of a Workflow. Table DW-6 demonstrates how to add a Send Email step into your Workflow.  Table WM-13 walks you through the steps to setup the Send Email Workflow Activity.

 

Table WM-13 Setting Up an Email Activity

Steps

Actions

1

Select the Notifications plug-in located within the Add Activities field.

2

Click the Send E-mail activity.

3

Click OK.*

4

Complete the required fields

5

Choose a dependency status Success. Your newly created workflow activity, Send Email, will depend on the successful completion of the previous workflow activity.

6

Click OK. The attributes of the relationship will appear in the Dependencies field.

 

* Note:  The Knowledge Base Server must be configured to send email in order for the Notify Activity Plug-ins to work.
