Creating a Workflow Service

Configuring s Workflow Services is very similar to that of creating Workflows, however, since the Services are stateless and cannot execute stand alone, they do not contain information pertaining to machine configuration, scheduling or continuous integration. The steps for configuring a Workflow Services follow the same process.

 

Table WM-14 Creating a Workflow

Steps

Actions

1

Navigate to the OM Workflow perspective. Make sure you can see the Workflow Service Explorer views. If not, go to Window->Show View to enable.

2

Right click on the Workflow Explorer tree and select New->Workflow Service or use the File->New->Workflow Service menu item.

3

Enter a  Workflow Service name and click OK.

4

Click on the Add Activities Tab.

5

Add desired Activities by clicking the Add Activity button

6

File->Save to save your results.
