Testing Your Installation with Workflows

 

To test your installation, we will walk you through executing your first Workflows using the sample Workflows provided with your installation. By following these Workflows, you will quickly understand how Workflows are defined and executed allowing a quick path to defining your own Workflows.  You will monitor your Workflows using the Workflow Monitor.
