Reports and Logs

 

The results, logs and reports of all Workflow and Workflow Activities can be viewed in both the Eclipse Rich Client Platform or the Management Control Console.  All reports and logs are centralized which helps teams stay in sync by providing on-demand access to Workflow and Build logs, reports, and metrics.
