Workflow Folders

 

By using the “Name” field on the “Configure” tab from the Workflow Editor, you can add a Folder structure as part of the name.  When a Folder structure is added in this way, the Workflow Explorer will display Folders based on the high-level structure name.  For example, if your Testing team choose to keep all of their Workflows in a “Testing” Folder they would define their Workflow Names as follows:

Testing/<Workflow Name>

The “/” (backslash) separates the Folder structure.
