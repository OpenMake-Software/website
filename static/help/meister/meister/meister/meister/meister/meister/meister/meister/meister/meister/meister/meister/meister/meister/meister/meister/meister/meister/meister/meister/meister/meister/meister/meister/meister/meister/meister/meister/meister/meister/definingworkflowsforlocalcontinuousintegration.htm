Defining Workflows for Local Continuous Integration

 

With your base installation, you can configure your Workflow to run with Continuous Integration without purchasing a Remote Agent license.  In this case, your local machine, referred to as “My Computer,” serves as your Remote Agent and Continuous Integration Server.  Your local machine also must host your Source Code Management tool that will trigger your continuous integration process.   

 

As you define your Workflow Activities each will be configured to run on “My Computer.”  When you execute your local Continuous Integration process, all Workflow Activities will execute on your local machine.
