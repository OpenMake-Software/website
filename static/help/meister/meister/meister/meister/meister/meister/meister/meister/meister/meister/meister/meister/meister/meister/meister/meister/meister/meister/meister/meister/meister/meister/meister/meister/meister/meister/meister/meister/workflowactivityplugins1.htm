Workflow Activity Plug-ins

 

Workflow Activity Plug-ins support the steps that are executed during a Workflow.  Standard Workflow Activity Plug-ins are provided which integrate with popular life cycle tools commonly used in the course of developing and deploying software applications. These Plug-ins can be used with little or no customization eliminating the need for each development team to re-invent the process each time a new Workflow is defined.
