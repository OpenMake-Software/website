Adding Activities to a Workflow Service

Adding Activities to a Workflow is very similar to adding Activities to a Workflow. The main difference is that Activities added to Workflow do not get added to a specific machine, but rather directly to the Service itself. Below are sample steps for adding a CVS Activity to an existing Workflow Service.

 

Table WM-15 Adding Activities to Workflow Service

Steps

Actions

1

Select the Activities tab.

2

Click the Add Activity button. A window appears with two fields: one for plug-ins (Activity Plug-in) and the other for activities (Activity Type).

3

Select the CVS plug-in from the Activity Plug-in field and then the Update from CVS activity from the Activity Type field.

4

Click OK. Your new activity name, Update from CVS $(JOB_NAME), will appear under the machine that you chose.
