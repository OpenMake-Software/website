Using Variables

 

When developing your customized Build Methods, it is important to remember that you must not use any hard coded values.   You want your Build Method to be reusable.  This reusability allows you to share Build Methods between developers and development teams without re-invinting the process. It also means that you can enforce build best practices and standards in a collabortive process, where everyone can comprehend how the build executes.
