Initial Login

 

After installation, and when the Eclipse RCP Client is started, a Login dialog box will be displayed.  Use your computer “login” name for initially logging into the RCP Client.  If you are using Windows, your computer “login” name is the same as what you enter when you boot Windows. For Unix or Linux environments, your computer “login” name is the same as your Unix or Linux account name. 

 

For the first time login, you can use any password.  The password that you initially enter will be saved on the Knowledgebase Server and encrypted for future logins.  You will be required to enter your Password twice to validate you have entered it correctly.
