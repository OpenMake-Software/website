ERROR 130:

          Could not find Build Service <Build Service Name> for < Target Name>

 

Description:

The Build Service associated with the Target Name is no longer valid.

Resolution:

The Build Service may have been deleted or renamed from the Knowledge base.  At the time your Target was created, the Build Service was valid.  This Build Service no longer exists.  Edit your Target and choose a new Build Service.  If no Build Service is appropriate for you Target, you may need to re-add your old Build Service to the Knowledge Base.
