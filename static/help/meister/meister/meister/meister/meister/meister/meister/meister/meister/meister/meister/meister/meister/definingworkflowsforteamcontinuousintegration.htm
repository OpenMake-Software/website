Defining Workflows for Team Continuous Integration

 

When defining Workflows to execute a Team Continuous Integration process, you may want to define the Workflow Activities to execute on a machine other than your local machine, “My Computer.”  This will allow you to distribute the Workflow Activities across multiple machines that are running an OpenMake Remote Agent. For this reason, it is important to install all Remote Agents that you will use to execute your Continuous Integration process before you define your Workflow.  Once you have installed your Remote Agents, you can then define your Workflow Activities to execute on the correct machine.
