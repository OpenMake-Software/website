OpenMake Meister Continuous Integration and Workflow Management

 

OpenMake Meister® includes a Continuous Integration server that aligns the sharing of operational tasks between developers and production control teams.  It creates a centralized process for defining the steps required for building, testing and deploying software.  By integrating with internally developed scripts and best of breed commercial and open source tools, Meister’s free CI component lets your teams jointly define the logic and scheduling of your software builds and deploys with centralized reports and trend analysis. You can upgrade Meister to include the use of Remote Agents to distribute the workload of your build, test and deploy steps across multiple machines and diverse operating systems. With the added efficiency of server pooling and centralized configuration management, Meister offers a truly enterprise Continuous Build solution.
