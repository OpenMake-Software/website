Build and CI Procedures and Usage

 

Builds, CI Activities and Workflows can be executed in a variety of methods. They can be executed on demand or added to a schedule for automation.   They can run parallelized or use multiple machines for distributed workload processing.  Because these terms are often used in different ways, below is a definition and review of common Build, CI Activities and Workflow procedures.  These definitions describe how Meister addresses these different Build, CI Activities and Workflow methods.
