Reports and Logs Organization

 

There are two classifications of Reports and Logs.  First there are the OpenMake Meister specific Reports and Logs. Secondly, there are Reports and Logs that are generated based on a Workflow Activity application life cycle plug-in.  Workflow Activity plug-in Reports and Logs are managed based on the Workflow Name and Run Number.  These Reports and Logs are different from the OpenMake Meister Reports and Logs as they are generated by external tools called by your Workflow. 

 

Reports and Logs that are unique to OpenMake Meister are managed in two ways.  First, they are displayed under the Workflow similar to a Workflow Activity plug-in Report or Log.  Secondly, OpenMake Meister Reports and Logs are managed and displayed by the Eclipse Rich Client Platform OM Reports Perspective.  Using the OM Reports Perspective you will see a Workflow Log Explorer and a Report Explorer.  The Workflow Log Explorer shows you all Workflow logs based on date, time and machine. Similarly, the Report Explorer shows you all Meister Reports based on date, time and machine. The following OpenMake Meister Reports can be found under the Report Explorer:

•     Meister Insight – Build Audit Reports and Impact Analysis Reports

•     Bill of Material – When an SCM tool is used, shows what files were checked out before a Build.

•     Differences – When an SCM tool is used, shows what files changed.

•     Workflows – Summary, Graphs and History – Show Metrics of Workflows.

•     Build Service Options – Displays all compile and Link configurations for Build Services.

•     Groups – Shows Users and their associated Groups.

•     Project and Dependencies – Shows a list of Projects and their defined Dependency Directories.

•     User Privileges – Shows all Users with associated privileges to Projects, Dependency Directories, Build Services and Public Workflows.
