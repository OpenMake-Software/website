Workflow Hierarchy Section

 

This section under the Activities Tab shows the order in which Workflow Activities will be executed and on which Server Pool. You can assign Server Pools to your entire Workflow, or to specific Workflow Activities. You can Add and Remove Workflow Activities from your Workflow.  Once completed, the Workflow Hierarchy section gives you a high level view of all steps which will be executed as part of your Workflow.  The steps at the top of the Workflow execute first, ending with the steps at the bottom of the Workflow Tree.
